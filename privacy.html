Privacy policy

Privacy policy

TAG Casts is built to keep your listening private. The app does not collect or transmit personal data, and it does not include ads, trackers, or analytics SDKs.

Data stored on your device

To work correctly, TAG Casts saves a small set of listening data locally, such as the shows you follow, tags, and your playback position so you can resume later. This information stays on your device.

Data sharing

TAG Casts does not share your listening data with the developer or any third parties. Your content and preferences remain local.

What data other parties may collect and store?

TAG Casts uses the public iTunes API to provide podcast search results. When you search, Apple may receive and store information such as the words in your query and your public IP address. Please refer to Apple’s privacy policy for details.
